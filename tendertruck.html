Ensuring applicators are supplied with products

When an applicator is applying, an ag retailer is making money. Unfortunately, they travel slowly and have relatively small tanks to hold the chemicals they apply.
‍
Tender trucks ensure that they are able to continuously apply throughout a day by regularly supplying them with new products from the shed.

Who drives a tender truck?

Accessibility is key!

These trucks are typically operated by retired, part-time employees who are not fans of technology. We needed to design an application that not only guaranteed efficient tender truck schedules, but was also so easy to use that an average tender truck driver could pick up an iPad and get started with minimal training.

Our application coordinates with the web app in the manager's office to supply the tender truck driver with easy-to-follow trips and navigation, helping them efficiently drive between the chemical shed and applicator.

Our solution

The use of our tender truck app has resulted in 15% improved travel time for drivers.
